Our Story

Passed down for generations, made with local Fillmore California ingredients. Our Chile Salsa has been a cherished family recipe, passed down from generation to generation. Made with the freshest and finest local ingredients sourced from the heart of Fillmore, California, our salsa is a true taste of the region. What sets our salsa apart is its savory and tangy flavor profile. We carefully select the ripest chilies, tomatoes, and spices to create a harmonious blend that tantalizes the taste buds. Whether you're a fan of mild or enjoy the heat of spicier salsas, we have a range of options to suit your preference. From our traditional family recipe to unique variations, there's a Chile Salsa for everyone.

Home Made

Each one of our salsas is produced using our family kitchen, ensuring the quality and flavor of our family recipe.

Locally Sourced

As a small business, we are proud to source our ingredients from our community here in Fillmore, California.
