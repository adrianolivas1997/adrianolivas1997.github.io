How its made

At Chile Salsa, we take pride in using the freshest and finest local ingredients sourced from Fillmore, California. Our salsa is made with the following ingredients, locally grown chilies, ripe tomatoes, spices and seasonings, and a secret family twist!

How to Serve

Here are some creative ideas on how to enjoy our authentic Mexican salsa roja recipe:

  • Drizzle it over huevos rancheros for a burst of flavor.
  • Pair it with totopos chips for a delightful snack.
  • Use it as a topping for queso con chile rojo.
  • Serve it alongside taco quesadillas for a zesty kick.
  • Enjoy it with tlacoyos topped with beans.
  • Enhance the flavor of carne asada huaraches with this salsa.
  • If you have leftovers, transform them into migas con huevo or chilaquiles rojos.

Get creative and explore the versatile ways in which you can savor the authentic flavors of our Mexican salsa roja.
